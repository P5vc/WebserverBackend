About

Priveasy is a community-driven internet privacy and security organization. Our goal is to provide you with—at the lowest cost possible—the tools and services you need, to stay safe online. We always build our tools upon the best, open source projects out there, and design them to guard your privacy and freedom of speech at all times. At no point will you find any trackers, cookies, analytics, etc. on our website or in our products.

Priveasy is committed to remaining open source, transparent, and as friendly as possible. If you ever have any questions, please don't hesitate to reach out! You can contact us at Support@Priveasy.org or via Signal, using the number provided in the footer. If you enjoy our services, please reach out to friends and family to let them know about us: the more users that join, the lower the costs will get for everyone!

VPN

Undoubtedly the most popular service we offer is our VPN. Built from the ground up with privacy and security in mind, we have one of the fastest and cheapest VPNs out there. When selecting a VPN service, it's crucial to pick one whom you can trust. Priveasy is proud to be the only 100% open source VPN provider out there. What does this mean? It means that we're powered by a whole community of people who have rigorously audited our code, ensuring optimal privacy and security practices. While other VPN providers are forced to hire third party companies to review their code in secret and vouch for them, we're the only VPN provider who posts our code online, allowing anyone and everyone to review it, contribute to it, and verify our claims.

The main VPN protocol we use is WireGuard. Our unique implementation maintains WireGuard's world-class speed and security, and improves upon it, adding features such as quantum resistance and a private LAN for all of your connected devices. Understanding that some of our users live, work, attend school, etc. in places where freedom of speech is often restricted, we also offer a Shadowsocks proxy (and optional v2ray plugin), allowing you to access the open internet from just about any device, anywhere in the world, without having to worry about your connection being blocked.

As always, our VPN comes with blazing-fast speeds, unlimited bandwidth, and a zero logging policy, so you can browse with peace of mind. When signing up, you have a choice between plan #2 and plan #3. Plan #2 comes with two domestic profiles, which is perfect for a casual user who owns just one or two devices. Plan #3 includes five domestic profiles and five offshore profiles, plus another five Shadowsocks connections (2 of which support v2ray-plugin). This option is the best choice for most people: it allows you to connect many different devices all at once, gives you options over server location, and enables traffic obfuscation, so that you may even connect from some of the most restrictive networks in the world. All of our domestic profiles are based out of the United States and will therefore be the quickest and easiest for a majority of our users. Our offshore profiles are based out of the Netherlands, and allow for activities such as torrenting and streaming, that aren't allowed with our domestic VPN.

Burner Email

Our burner email service provides you with unique, alternative email addresses to give out to untrustworthy websites or people. These burner email addresses will automatically forward any emails sent to them to the personal email address of your choice, without ever revealing the final destination. Therefore, should a company with one of these email addresses sell your information or start spamming you, you can simply "burn" (delete) the forwarder, and they'll no longer be able to contact you.

In addition to the email forwarders, with plans #2 and #3 we also provide the capability to send emails from a burner address. The ability to send emails from an address other than your own can be essential to protecting your privacy online, such as when responding to verification emails from untrustworthy sites, setting-up meetings with online sellers, or simply trying to avoid having your email added to one of the many online spam lists. For those of you with plan #3, you'll notice that you have the added capability to send burner emails from one of your forwarding addresses, allowing you to both send and receive emails from the same burner address.

Text Messaging

Do you ever need to send yourself or someone else a quick reminder? Need to contact a company or someone you just met online but don't want them having your personal number to spam or sell? With plan #3 you can easily send-out text messages from our number, instead of having to use your own, further preserving your privacy.

Pastebin

With your account you get up to 100 million characters-worth of free storage space to save notes, code snippets, and anything else, to your heart's content. You can choose to keep these to yourself, or (with plans #2 and #3) post them publicly, and get an immediate link to share with the world.

URL Shortening

We at Priveasy are lucky-enough to own a two character domain, allowing us to provide URL shortening services to all of our customers. We can take any long URL, and turn it into the same length (12 characters) as our main URL: Priveasy.org. Those with plan #3 will also have access to click statistics, so they can see just how many times their link has been used.
